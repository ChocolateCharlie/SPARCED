# SPARCED panitumumab

This model is based on the SPARCED standard model, to which panitumumab is
added.

Kd = 5.26E-11 based on the mean between the FDA's pharmacology review for
panitumumab (2005), Patel et al. (2010) and Spiegelberg et al. (2021).
kon was aligned on the kon of EGF binding EGFR.
